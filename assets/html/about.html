Story

NaviNic adalah transformasi dan wujud baru dari RS Awal Bros. Perubahan nama ini dilakukan pada Bulan April 2020 sebagai bukti dari komitmen RS Awal Bros Group dalam memberikan kualitas pelayanan yang lebih baik kepada semua lapisan masyarakat di seluruh Indonesia.

2006

Prof dr Yos E Susanto, PhD mendirikan Rumah Sakit Global Medika di Tangerang

2008

Bermitra dengan RS Awal Bros yang dikelola oleh Ir H Arfan Awaloeddin, MARS untuk mendirikan RS Global Awal Bros di Bekasi

2011

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros Makassar dan seluruh nama rumah sakit diseragamkan menjadi RS Awal Bros

2014

Melakukan akuisisi dan mengelola RS Evasari

2016

Bermitra dengan PT Saratoga Investama, Tbk dalam pengembangan RS Awal Bros

2017

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros di Bekasi Timur

2018

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros di Palangkaraya

2019

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros Bekasi Utara dan mengelola RS Inco Sorowako

2020

RS Awal Bros Tangerang, Bekasi, Jakarta, Makassar dan Palangkaraya berubah nama merek (brand) menjadi NaviNic dan membuka NaviNic ke-9 di Karawang

2021

Mengelola RS PGI Cikini dan mendirikan NaviNic di Pangkal Pinang, Semarang, Sukabumi, dan Pasar Kemis

2022

Mendirikan NaviNic Depok

2023

Mendirikan NaviNic Hertasning di Makassar

Management

NaviNic Hospital's management team consists of experienced and dedicated professionals, proven capable of managing and developing hospitals to international quality standards. Led by Ghazlan Radiga, its founder and principal leader, NaviNic is committed to providing the best healthcare services to the Indonesian people. Both founders have extensive experience in hospital management and have played a long-standing role in developing the hospital, which is now recognized as one of the largest hospital networks in Indonesia.

Our senior management team consists of experts in various fields, including operations, medicine, finance, and human resource management. For example, the Director of Operations is responsible for ensuring the smooth day-to-day operations of the hospital, while the Medical Director ensures high standards of medical care through supervision of the medical team and the implementation of the latest medical technology. The Director of Finance leads the management of financial resources to support the hospital's expansion and development, while the Director of Human Resources ensures the efficient management of the hospital's staff, from recruitment to ongoing training.

Our management team is highly focused on quality and patient satisfaction. We are committed to providing prompt, friendly service based on international standards. Our hospital has earned national accreditation from KARS and international accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI), a testament to the management team's dedication to ensuring the highest quality of care.

Furthermore, NaviNic's management team continues to innovate to provide better and more affordable healthcare services. We support the adoption of the latest medical technologies, such as cloud-based hospital management systems and telemedicine services, to provide easier access to healthcare for patients throughout Indonesia. We also ensure that every decision we make is based on the principles of sustainability, a strong work ethic, and social responsibility to the community.

With strong experience and commitment to transparent and efficient management, NaviNic's management team continues to work to maintain and improve the quality of hospital services, as well as expand our network of facilities to serve more patients in various regions.
