About Us

Primaya Hospital is committed to providing quality services that prioritize patient safety and security. Our hospitals have been nationally accredited by the Hospital Accreditation Commission (KARS), and two of our hospitals have received international accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI) since 2014.

By 2023, Primaya Hospital Group has presented 16 hospitals strategically located and spread across various regions in Indonesia, with 2000+ inpatient beds, and supported by 3800+ experienced professional medical personnel including 900+ specialist doctors. Primaya Hospital is equipped with advanced technology such as 128 CT Scan with AI Tech, 1.5 Tesla MRI, Cath lab with Coronary Angiography, Rotablator, Endovenous Laser Therapy.

Primaya Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services with four main services: maternal and child care, heart and blood vessel care, trauma/orthopedics, and cancer. Primaya Hospital continues to develop several other services, including urology, brain and nerve care, and eye care, to become its leading services in the future.

To meet patient needs, the Primaya Hospital Group also provides other healthcare services such as clinical laboratories, homecare, telemedicine, a fertility clinic, and medical evacuation. Going forward, the Primaya Hospital Group will continue to grow both in quality and quantity.
