About Us

NaviNic is committed to providing quality services that prioritize patient safety and security. Our hospitals have been nationally accredited by the Hospital Accreditation Commission (KARS), and two of our hospitals have received international accreditation from the Joint Commission International (JCI) since 2014.

ㅤBy 2023, NaviNic Group has presented 16 hospitals strategically located and spread across various regions in Indonesia, with 2000+ inpatient beds, and supported by 3800+ experienced professional medical personnel including 900+ specialist doctors. NaviNic is equipped with advanced technology such as 128 CT Scan with AI Tech, 1.5 Tesla MRI, Cath lab with Coronary Angiography, Rotablator, Endovenous Laser Therapy.

NaviNic provides comprehensive healthcare services with four main services: maternal and child care, heart and blood vessel care, trauma/orthopedics, and cancer. NaviNiccontinues to develop several other services, including urology, brain and nerve care, and eye care, to become its leading services in the future.

ㅤTo meet patient needs, the NaviNic Group also provides other healthcare services such as clinical laboratories, homecare, telemedicine, a fertility clinic, and medical evacuation. Going forward, the NaviNic Group will continue to grow both in quality and quantity.

Why NaviNic Hospital?

Nationally (KARS) and Internationally (JCI) Accredited

NaviNic provides excellent service by prioritizing the quality of patient safety and security, which is reflected in NaviNic's national accreditation by the Hospital Accreditation Commission (KARS) and two of NaviNic's hospitals have been internationally accredited by the Joint Commission International (JCI).

Good Health Services

NaviNic provides comprehensive healthcare services for both Indonesians and foreign nationals. NaviNic serves patients with individual payment methods, company coverage, insurance, or the National Health Insurance (BPJS). NaviNic provides services with advanced technology and quality tailored to the community's needs. NaviNic will provide healthcare solutions for the community.

Ease of Access

To meet the health needs of the community, NaviNic Group is spread across several regions and major cities in Indonesia with strategic and easily accessible locations so that the community's need for health services can be met properly.

Complete Facilities

NaviNic offers comprehensive services including Emergency Room, Radiology, Laboratory, and Pharmacy services, all available 24/7. Furthermore, NaviNic boasts a spacious parking area, patient education room, comfortable polyclinics, lactation room, play area in the Children's Polyclinic, ATM Center, prayer room, Wi-Fi for patients' families, canteen, and a comfortable lobby area. NaviNic offers superior services for its patients, including the Heart and Blood Vessels Center, Maternal and Child Services, Trauma Services, and Oncology (Cancer) Services.

Story

NaviNic adalah transformasi dan wujud baru dari RS Awal Bros. Perubahan nama ini dilakukan pada Bulan April 2020 sebagai bukti dari komitmen RS Awal Bros Group dalam memberikan kualitas pelayanan yang lebih baik kepada semua lapisan masyarakat di seluruh Indonesia.

2006

Prof dr Yos E Susanto, PhD mendirikan Rumah Sakit Global Medika di Tangerang

2008

Bermitra dengan RS Awal Bros yang dikelola oleh Ir H Arfan Awaloeddin, MARS untuk mendirikan RS Global Awal Bros di Bekasi

2011

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros Makassar dan seluruh nama rumah sakit diseragamkan menjadi RS Awal Bros

2014

Melakukan akuisisi dan mengelola RS Evasari

2016

Bermitra dengan PT Saratoga Investama, Tbk dalam pengembangan RS Awal Bros

2017

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros di Bekasi Timur

2018

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros di Palangkaraya

2019

Mendirikan RS Awal Bros Bekasi Utara dan mengelola RS Inco Sorowako

2020

RS Awal Bros Tangerang, Bekasi, Jakarta, Makassar dan Palangkaraya berubah nama merek (brand) menjadi NaviNic dan membuka NaviNic ke-9 di Karawang

2021

Mengelola RS PGI Cikini dan mendirikan NaviNic di Pangkal Pinang, Semarang, Sukabumi, dan Pasar Kemis

2022

Mendirikan NaviNic Depok

2023

Mendirikan NaviNic Hertasning di Makassar

Management

Despite the name change, the shareholding composition of NaviNic Group remains the same as that of the hospital known as RS Awal Bros Group. The founders of NaviNic Group are Prof. Dr. Yos E. Susanto, PhD, and Ir. H. Arfan Awaloeddin. The founders will continue to guarantee their experience and management strength, enabling NaviNic to become one of the largest hospital networks in Indonesia.
